TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE
----------------------

Frontend Components:
├── multiStepBooking.tsx (Container)
│   ├── customerInfoForm.tsx (Step 1)
│   ├── servicesSelection.tsx (Step 2)
│   ├── meetingLocation.tsx (Step 3)
│   └── dateTimeSelection.tsx (Step 4)
│
Backend API:
├── get_appointment_services()
│   └── Returns: List of active services
│
└── book_time_slot()
    ├── Accepts: All customer data
    └── Creates: Event with custom fields

Database:
├── Appointment Service (DocType)
│   ├── service_name
│   ├── description
│   ├── duration
│   ├── price
│   └── enabled
│
└── Event (Custom Fields)
    ├── custom_customer_phone
    ├── custom_selected_services
    ├── custom_location_type
    ├── custom_our_location
    └── custom_customer_location
